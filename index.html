DF-XNA

An Open Source Clone of Dwarf Fortress in C# with XNA 4.0.

What is it?

DF-XNA is my own adaptation of Bay 12 Games' (Tarn and Zach Adams) Dwarf Fortress in C#.

Why make this?

Unfortunately, the original Dwarf Fortress is a closed source endeavor - meaning aspiring modders or interested parties are confined soley to the modification of the Rawfiles.

So I figured - why not make my own version for myself and others to enjoy?

Will this be an exact clone?

No, it will not be. Since the original code is completely closed, I'm flying blind on a large majority of internal processes and properties. Therefore, some will be modified or outright replaced in some fashion.

When will it be done?

When it's done!

Honestly, there's no estimated timeline because of how large an undertaking this is.

Are you crazy?

More than certainly!
